OTHANDWENI CHILDREN'S HOME

WELCOME TO THE OTHANDWENI CHILDREN'S HOME WEBSITE!

"Family is not about whose blood you carry. It's about who you love and who loves you back"

Othandweni, which means "a Place of Love", is a place where children are loved and given opportunities to grow and display their potential(Givengain,2001). Situated in Mofolo South,Soweto. Othandweni Children's Centre offers residential care for children who have been abused, abandoned or neglected(givengain,2001). A centre that was founded in 1984, caters for 90 children from the early ages of birth to 18 years(Givengain,2001). The centre also provides a limited space for destitute mothers who are contemplating the futures of their unborn children(Givengain,2001).
