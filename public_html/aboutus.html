OTHANDWENI CHILDREN'S HOME

What is the home all about?

Othandweni Children's Home is a place of safety for children of the ages of 0 - 18 years old. We provide adequate care,housing and education through the help of the government,charities and volunteers(individuals & businesses). The devoted workers are there 24/7 try to ensure that all the children of Othandweni feel loved,safe and have a healthy environment to call home.

Vision Statement

Our home aims to aid children in realizing their full potential, as well as ensuring that the children are reintegrated as healthy,lively,responsible and independent adults in their communities.

Mission Statement

Othandweni Children's Home operates with local authorities and members of the community to provides a chance at life to orphans and other children. We aim to give our children basic education,health services and an opportunity to grow up in a healthy,safe,caring and familial environment.

Background info

  • We provide food,shelter,health services and basic education to all our children
  • The home was re-opened in 2014 due to unfulfilled government orders and financial reasons.As a result, the home was unable to provide for the children,however, it is currently under close supervision to ensure that history does not repeat itself(Givengain,2001).
